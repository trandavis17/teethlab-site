The science

Advanced laser whitening, gentle on your smile.

Our triple-threat, advanced laser system offers a gentle yet effective solution to an age-old problem — stained teeth. Every option is designed for the utmost safety of your gums, enamel, and existing dental work, with an exclusive mineral-rich formula that enriches your enamel.

  • Zero to minimal sensitivity — 90% report none
  • Safe for gums, enamel & existing dental work
